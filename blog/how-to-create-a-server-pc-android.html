How to Create a Server (PC & Android)

Windows:
If you have a connection to one WI-FI, you just need to find out your Local IPv4 You can find it in the router (DHCP Client List), or by writing ipconfig in cmd

If you are alone, you can use ZeroTier, it is available for Windows/Android\IOS and others, then create your own network there and connect to it, then copy its IPv4

Android:

The method is similar to Windows, look in the router for what local IPv4 you have in the DHCP Client List

If you do not have access to the Internet then via Hotspot, One of you distributes Hotspot,  the second one goes to WI-FI connects to Hotspot, The Device that is connected has an IP, which you can find in the settings
